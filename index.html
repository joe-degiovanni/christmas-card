2022 Review

We moved!

It was a big adjustment from our 1/3 acre lot in Sartell to 40 acres in St. Joseph. We are lucky to have Jim and Mary living next to us to help train us into living on the farm. Our house sold in June and it was fun watching construction of the new house from foundation to finish over the summer. We're super excited, but have a lot to learn!

Our new address is:

12822 County Road 51
Saint Joseph MN 56374

We traveled!

Kristina and I got to travel to Colorado with Mike and Erin to Golden, Colorado for a live Jason Isbell performance at Red Rocks. We had a lot of fun over the 4th of July holiday with our friends in Wisconsin Dells.

The highlight of our travels was bringing the whole family to England to visit the Bowerman family. Missing Hannah and Lewis's wedding in 2021 was a huge disappointment, but we were so excited to meet their baby boy George after finally being able to travel there this year! We owe a Yuuuuuge "Thank you!" to Mary and Paul for being amazing hosts despite the chaos we unleashed on their lovely English home village :-) Rachel was an amazing tour guide as well! We can't wait to come back and visit again!
